Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world and are native to South America. They are known for their chill personalities and love for water.

Top 5 Fun Facts About Capybaras

  1. They can grow over 4 feet long!
  2. They live in large social groups.
  3. Capybaras are excellent swimmers.
  4. They eat mostly grasses and aquatic plants.
  5. They communicate using a variety of sounds!
